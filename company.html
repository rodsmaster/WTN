COMPANY DESCRIPTION

The amount of data being created and stored on a global level is almost inconceivable. And this amount just keeps increasing. Given these statistics we created What is The Nuvem Solutions. a company that was created in 2017. It was born basically of the expression "time is money" in order to satisfy the information storage demand of the world. Our industry has grown exponentially high due to the fervor of the backup, storage and storage market. We maintain a competitive edge in the market through the excellent work, generating reduction of energy consumption, space and the use of air conditioning. We are strategically installed in an area of 150,000 MT² our highlight is due to the cooling system, which provides thermal energy to various places in the region. Our aim is to meet the growing demand through the purchase of modern equipment, which will enable the prospecting of new customers in a broader scope, greater ability to adapt to customer needs, lower costs and reduced process time.
